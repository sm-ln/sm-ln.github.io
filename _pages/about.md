I am Shimin Wang, currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Division of Industrial Data Science at Lingnan University in Hong Kong, and an Adjunct with MIT. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral researcher working with Prof. Richard D. Braatz and Prof. Martin Guay.

My research operates at the exact intersection of **Control Engineering, Machine Learning, and Mechanical System Dynamics**. 

I am particularly interested in areas such as:
- State estimation and output regulation
- Learning-based control for complex systems
- High relative degree systems (e.g., magnetic levitation systems, distillation columns, and flexible joint robots)

Feel free to reach out to me for academic discussions or collaborations.
